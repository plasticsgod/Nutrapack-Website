How we work

Sketch to shelf.

One partner across the whole package. Five steps, no hand-offs, no broken phone — just a clear path from your first idea to a finished product on a pallet.

01
Step one

Brief

Send us a sketch, a competitor you admire, or a Pinterest board. We translate whatever you have into a real packaging spec — materials, dimensions, finishes, decoration method. No NDA needed to get started.

If you don't know what you want yet, that's fine too. Most founders don't. We ask the right questions to get there.

What you sendAnything What you get backA spec sheet TimeSame week
02
Step two

Design

Our in-house design studio delivers everything you need to get to manufacturing: dielines, label artwork, 3D mockups, pre-press files. Two rounds of revisions are included with every project — no surprise creative invoices.

If you already have a designer, we work with their files. If you don't, we are your designer.

DeliverablesPress-ready files RevisionsTwo included Time~14 days
03
Step three

Source

This is where most boutique brands lose weeks. We consolidate every component — bottles, caps, labels, secondaries, fillers — under one timeline and one purchase order. No three-vendor scramble. No timing risk landing on you.

You get one quote, one PO, one timeline.

POsOne Vendors managedBy us Your jobApprove and ship
04
Step four

Produce

Press-pass approval before the run goes wide. Line QA throughout production. A sealed sample box mailed to your office before anything ships, so the first time you see your finished product isn't on a pallet at your fulfillment partner.

QAPress-pass + line Sample boxAlways
05
Step five

Ship

Direct to your fulfillment partner, your co-packer, or your warehouse. We track every pallet from line to dock, and we don't disappear after delivery. If something is wrong on arrival, we're the one phone call you make.

TrackedPallet-level After deliveryOne point of contact
