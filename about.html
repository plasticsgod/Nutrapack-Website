About NutraPack

One partner for the whole package.

We're a packaging studio and sourcing operation built specifically for the founders big suppliers won't pick up the phone for. We design, source, and ship custom packaging components for emerging supplement, wellness and cosmetic brands — starting at 10,000 MOQ.

Why we started.

Founders kept telling us the same story. Beautiful idea, real demand, ready to launch — but every supplier conversation ended at "we don't take orders under 50,000 units." Or "design isn't included." Or "labels are a different vendor."

Sourcing the right materials and executing on tight timelines became a second job most founders didn't sign up for. Multiple suppliers, conflicting lead times, decoration quality that didn't match samples, components arriving on different weeks — small complexities that quietly killed launches.

Smart founders were getting buried in logistics that had nothing to do with the product they were trying to launch. So we built the partner we wished existed.

How we're different.

We handle the print production end-to-end — taking your artwork through press matching, label printing, and quality control. And we consolidate the full component stack under one PO so you don't manage three vendors and pray the timing works.

No real minimum. Every order is custom-tailored — sourced, printed, shipped, and priced for what you actually need.

What we believe

Three things we won't compromise.

01

Real humans, real quotes

No form-letter bots, no ticket queues. Every brief gets a real human reply with materials, MOQs, lead times and price — within 48 hours.

02

Print production handled

Send us your artwork; we get it printed, decorated, and quality-checked across our supplier network. You ship a finished product, not a project plan.

03

One PO, no scramble

We source the full component stack — bottles, caps, labels, secondaries — and consolidate it under one timeline and one purchase order.
